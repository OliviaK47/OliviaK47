Favorite Brand

The brand I decided to look at is Mysteries of Mayhem. They sell home fragrances and bath and body products. Some of the types of things they sell are candles, wax melts, body wash, bubble bath, car fresheners and so much more. There is a range of fragrances to choose from, including seasonal smells. They also have smells that are inspired by video games and movies.

Why this Brand?

I really enjoy the products this brand has to offer but I also enjoy looking at the website. The website is not super unique but it is very clean and professional looking. Their formatting for their webpage is typically a white background and black text. The basic background allows for the pictures of the products to be more than just the product. In the pictures of the candles there is always something else in the picture, whether it is blurred lights in the background or a fake skeleton hand. I like the way these pictures look because it draws your eye to them. The fonts that were used also makes it look simple yet elevated.  

 What do I Plan to Use?  

I like the fonts that Mysteries of Mayhem chose to use. They use a combination of fonts throughout their website. All of the fonts have a similar look to them but are also slightly different. As of right now, I plan to use the fonts that they chose. They are all google fonts making them easy to find. I am not too sure which font is on the header but I believe it is Segoe UI. They also use helvetica neue and sans serif, along with a few others. I believe I want my header to be in segoe UI, and my body paragraphs sans serif. I think I want to use their style approach of a white background and black texts. If I do not stick with their website style, I still plan on using a lighter color for the background and a dark color on the text. On the homepage they have their logo on the top of the page and some pictures of their products slideshowing behind it. They also have their logo at the top of every page on their website.  

My Logo Header 

    For my header I wanted it to be simple still like the Mysteries of Mayhem website but also still have a pop of color. So I decided  to use a basic yellow banner. I like the simplicity of their website and wanted to bring that over to my website.
